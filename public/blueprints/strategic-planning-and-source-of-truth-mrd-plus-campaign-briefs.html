1 Process Overview

Centralizes positioning, competitive intelligence, market research, and campaign performance into a governed, queryable platform for content strategists and copywriters. Produces validated campaign MRDs and campaign briefs with automated quality scoring, replacing the multi-revision cycles between C&C and requesting teams that delay every downstream creative process.

Primary Outcome
Single governed gateway for messaging, MRDs, and briefs
Scale
~60 to 70 content strategists plus PMM, FM, DS
Time Savings Target
50 to 60% efficiency gain on research and brief development
Future Reusability
HIGH. PMM, field marketing, demand strategy, events

3 End to End Process

Closed loop process. Signals from source systems flow through the AI stages and feed back into upstream models.

01

AGGREGATE

Ingest and unify internal sources (Seismic, MRDs, brand/legal guidelines, RevTech/AEP) and external sources (competitive intelligence, market research) into a governed gateway with automated refresh.

02

ANALYZE

Surface audience insights by segment (industry, persona, product), competitive positioning, and historical campaign performance on demand through a strategist query interface.

03

GENERATE

AI-assisted messaging development and campaign brief drafting grounded in the unified layer, with a reusable prompt and methodology library capturing what works.

04

VALIDATE

Synthetic audience testing of messaging against audience models before committing campaign spend, addressing the $2M-campaign-without-$20K-testing gap.

05

LEARN

Capture campaign performance and organizational intelligence in a feedback loop, building collective knowledge rather than losing it to individual memory.

06

ENABLE

Expose the layer via API and connectors so downstream tools (Gen Studio, execution teams, self-serve creation) can access validated messaging and audience.

Net-New Initiative Designs
Reimagined ways of working and new processes, not just optimizing what works today.
Full Requirements Documentation
Business rules, data and technology dependencies, potential role implications, and governance.
Prioritized V1 Capabilities
Sequenced backlog with defined V1 scope per initiative: what to build first, what to phase.

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Many foundational elements exist (Seismic content indexed, MRDs/personas built, brand guidelines in progress, RevTech/AEP providing audience), but a gateway with governed access has not been built.
  • Internal integration (client engagement patterns) is harder than external (public competitive).
  • Complete data source inventory needed before build.
  • Baseline hours must be measured for ROI.
  • Centralized location for non-IBM owned data sources is unresolved.

KPIs

  • Reduction in hours per messaging or research cycle (baseline TBD)
  • Percent of strategists using platform as primary research tool
  • Message consistency score across campaigns (methodology TBD)
  • Quality score of the brief
  • Whether the brief we are getting is understood
  • Number of revisions per brief
