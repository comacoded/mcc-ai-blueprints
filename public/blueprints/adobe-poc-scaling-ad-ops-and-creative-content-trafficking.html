1 Process Overview

Migrates end to end execution from home-grown ad tech to standard, automated systems integrated with both IBM tech and agency platforms. Maximizes speed to market, ensures data and creative quality, and provides measurable ad performance for future optimization and audience refinement.

Primary Outcome
Standardized ad ops with measurable performance feedback
Scale
Full funnel media creative across all paid channels
Time Savings Target
Reduces manual trafficking, audience prep, and reporting
Future Reusability
HIGH. ABM intent activation, predictive LTV, retargeting

3 End to End Process

Closed loop process. Signals from source systems flow through the AI stages and feed back into upstream models.

01

INGEST

Connect Real-Time CDP to ISC, web analytics, EPM and Datorama, ABM signals, MTA outputs, deployed IBM Ad Information, economic news, competitive ad insights, agency feedback mechanisms, and 3p intent signals.

02

SEGMENT

Build MTA-informed audience segments, suppress low-propensity, amplify high-performing cohorts, and make recommendations for MTA-informed segments.

03

ACTIVATE

Push segments natively to agency channels (search, social, display) via Adobe connectors to agency systems.

04

OPTIMIZE

Feed MTA insights into closed-loop dashboard. Surface reallocation recommendations to agency, enable ad recommendations based on recent past performance, and recommend ad variations based on recent performance trends.

Compressed 4-week plan. Outcome targets: net-new initiative designs, full requirements documentation (business rules, data and tech dependencies, role implications, governance), and a prioritized V1 capability backlog.

Net-New Initiative Designs
Reimagined ways of working and new processes, not just optimizing what works today.
Full Requirements Documentation
Business rules, data and technology dependencies, potential role implications, and governance.
Prioritized V1 Capabilities
Sequenced backlog with defined V1 scope per initiative: what to build first, what to phase.

Reimagine
Activities

  • Data source map (Adobe AEP + RT-CDP, MTA outputs, EPM, Datorama, industry and competitive feeds)

  • Requirements documentation (business rules, data and tech dependencies, governance)
Week 3
Prioritization and Sequencing

  • Validate designs with functional leaders and process owners (Marketing Automation, Ad Operations, Agency, Analytics)

  • Requirements for V1 scope finalized

  • Initial role impact assessment by domain
  • Sequenced capabilities to reach final state
Week 4
Finalize

  • Sequence V1 functionality / capabilities per initiative
  • Align on build-first vs phase vs defer
  • Final architecture sign-off with CIO
Deliverables

  • Sequenced V1 backlog per initiative

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Pending inflight Adobe solution design. Agency receives structured files; this is a protocol shift, not new capability.
  • MTA model is mature with channel/tactic outputs. Gap is operationalizing into the feedback loop.
  • Agency must adopt API-based ingestion and a structured MTA optimization playbook.
  • Audience and content taxonomy must be standardized across legacy and Adobe.
  • PII governance rules for segment activation via ad platforms.

KPIs

  • Campaign cycle time
  • CTRs / conversion rates
  • ROAS
  • Error rate in ads
  • Validation / QA effectiveness
  • Reduction in manual trafficking effort
