1 Process Overview

Analyzes content assets in AEM and auto-generates structured taxonomy tags, meta classifications, and content attributes aligned to the AEP layer and CJA dimension schema. Produces unified, machine-readable content taxonomy enabling downstream automation, personalization, and content recommendations at scale across all digital channels. Foundational prerequisite for ibm.com.

Primary Outcome
Unified taxonomy across ibm.com content
Scale
All ibm.com content (pages, PDFs, case studies, product docs)
Time Savings Target
Eliminates manual tagging, CJA segment creation, taxonomy maintenance
Future Reusability
HIGH. Foundation for personalization, chatbot, recommendations

3 End to End Process

Closed loop process. Signals from source systems flow through the AI stages and feed back into upstream models.

01

CLASSIFY

Auto-analyze content and assign multi-taxonomy tags (product, solution, industry, persona, funnel stage) on publish.

02

MAP

Convert taxonomy tags to CJA dimensions, eliminating manual custom segment creation for every campaign.

03

VALIDATE

Cross-reference tags against taxonomy rules, flag conflicts from acquisition content (Confluent, HashiCorp).

04

EXTEND

Propose taxonomy schema extensions when new products or acquisitions introduce content that doesn't fit existing categories.

05

AUDIT

Monitor ibm.com for untagged, stale-tagged, or inconsistently classified content and queue for remediation.

06

ACTIVATE

Push tagged content to downstream consumers: chatbot, personalization engine, search index, content syndication.

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Critical: Content Hub implementation timeline (Think 2026 dependency).
  • HIGH: Frontier model access policy.
  • HIGH: Adobe talent scarcity. We don't have a lot of Adobe-native people.
  • MEDIUM: Cross-team taxonomy governance (comms, brand, PMM, sales enablement).
  • Without ibm.com taxonomy in place, downstream personalization, chatbot, and recommendations cannot scale.

KPIs

  • Percent of ibm.com content with complete taxonomy tags
  • Time from content publish to tag availability in CJA
  • Manual CJA segment creation eliminated
  • Accuracy of tagging and meta
  • Searchability and discoverability of content
  • Consistency of taxonomy across systems
