1 Process Overview

Automates purchase order entry by ingesting SOWs, quotes, and contracts and provides intelligent allocation guidance across tactics and quarters. Ingests downstream performance to correlate spend with outcomes and produces ROI investment insights, creating a single source of truth connecting marketing spend to measurable business results.

Primary Outcome
Automated PO entry and spend-to-outcome correlation
Scale
All marketing spend across MMAPI, Events, Demand, Field
Time Savings Target
Eliminates manual PO entry and reconciliation
Future Reusability
VERY HIGH. Predictive spend, vendor scoring, contract intelligence

3 End to End Process

Closed loop process. Signals from source systems flow through the AI stages and feed back into upstream models.

01

INGEST

Extracts structured data from SOWs, quotes, and contracts (PDF/email); auto-populates PO fields and line items.

02

ALLOCATE

Intelligent allocation engine suggests spend distribution across tactics, channels, and quarters based on historical patterns and campaign plans.

03

TRACK

Real-time spend tracking links POs to invoices, actuals, and commitments; flags variances and over/under-spending.

04

CORRELATE

Downstream performance (leads, pipeline, revenue) is matched to spend records; AI produces ROI and efficiency metrics by tactic and channel.

05

INSIGHT

Dashboards and alerts surface spend-to-outcome correlations, identify high/low-performing investments, and recommend reallocation opportunities.

Reimagined ways of working and new processes, not just optimizing what works today.
Full Requirements Documentation
Business rules, data and technology dependencies, potential role implications, and governance.
Prioritized V1 Capabilities
Sequenced backlog with defined V1 scope per initiative: what to build first, what to phase.

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Historical spend-to-performance linkage may be incomplete; requires cleanup before AI can generate reliable ROI insights.
  • Performance correlation requires consistent UTM and campaign taxonomy across all channels.
  • Finance and procurement system API access and data sharing agreements needed.
  • Extraction from SOWs and contracts is a proven AI capability but requires tuning for IBM contract templates.

KPIs

  • Budget planning cycle time
  • Forecast accuracy versus actuals
  • Domain leader satisfaction score
  • Reduction in PO entry time
