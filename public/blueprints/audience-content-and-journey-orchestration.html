1 Workflow Overview

Unified workflow combining audience segmentation, journey, and content orchestration. Maps content to buyer-journey stages, driven by audience and enabled by taxonomy. Dynamically constructs audience segments based on behavioral patterns, mapping content activation across potential for value and velocity for a SaaS-driven product set.

Primary Outcome
AI-driven multi-channel journeys mapped to audience and content
Scale
All Demand Strategy, PMM, Field, Marketing Automation
Time Savings Target
Reduces journey build from weeks to days
Future Reusability
VERY HIGH. Cross-sell, lifecycle, partner-through, event nurture

3 End to End Workflow

Closed loop workflow. Signals from source systems flow through the AI stages and feed back into upstream models.

01

SEGMENT

Dynamically builds audience segments in Real-Time CDP using behavioral signals, firmographic, product interest, and engagement recency, scored by account value potential and deal velocity.

02

MAP

Maps each segment to buyer-journey stages (awareness to consideration to decision to expansion) and identifies optimal content mix per stage using historical conversion and taxonomy tags.

03

ORCHESTRATE

Auto-assembles multi-channel journeys in Journey Optimizer (email, web, paid, sales plays, partner enablement), with content pulled from AEM DAM based on persona, stage, and locale alignment.

04

LOCALIZE

Identifies market-specific content gaps, triggers localization workflows, and adapts messaging and asset selection for regional audiences and partner channels.

05

OPTIMIZE

Continuously monitors journey performance, surfaces content gaps and fatigue signals, and recommends journey modifications, feeding insights back into segmentation models.

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Adobe AEP, Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, Marketo, AEM all in-stack but require configuration for unified orchestration.
  • Content taxonomy must be mature and consistently applied across AEM DAM for AI-driven content-to-stage matching to work.
  • Risk: audience segment definitions vary across teams; standardization of segmentation logic and account scoring models is a prerequisite.
  • Risk: partner channel orchestration requires data-sharing agreements and integration with Salesforce Partner Cloud that may not be in place.
  • Unified segmentation taxonomy must be agreed across Demand Strategy, PMM, and regional teams before automated orchestration.

KPIs

  • Level of personalization (towards hyper-personalized)
  • Speed to activate journeys
  • Effectiveness of audience targeting
  • Seamlessness of journey orchestration across channels
