1 Workflow Overview

Expands the GenStudio POC to all supported ad formats. Generates channel-specific creative derivatives, runs automated brand and legal compliance scans, produces localized translations, and routes through human-in-the-loop approval gates. MMAPI variant simplifies derivative creation against templates and feeds attribution insights back to creative characteristics for future ads.

Primary Outcome
Automated derivative creation across all priority ad formats
Scale
All paid media channels and locales
Time Savings Target
Significant reduction in manual resizing, brand QA, legal review, localization
Future Reusability
VERY HIGH. White papers, event collateral, emails, sales enablement

3 End to End Workflow

Closed loop workflow. Signals from source systems flow through the AI stages and feed back into upstream models.

01

INGEST

Creative assets uploaded to AEM. Brand guidelines, legal rules, and locale requirements loaded as agent guardrails.

02

GENERATE

Agentic AI auto-produces channel derivatives across additional priority ad formats, prioritized based on media spend.

03

CHECK BRAND

Agent validates each derivative against brand guidelines and flags deviations for review.

04

CHECK LEGAL

Agent scans for legal and regulatory compliance and routes exceptions for human review.

05

TRANSLATE

Agent localizes copy and adapts visuals per market. Routes to in-market reviewer for approval.

06

VALIDATE

Human-in-the-loop approval at each gate before activation to media channels.

4 4-Week Co-Design Phase

Compressed 4-week plan. Outcome targets: net-new initiative designs, full requirements documentation (business rules, data and tech dependencies, role implications, governance), and a prioritized V1 capability backlog.

Net-New Initiative Designs
Reimagined ways of working and new workflows, not just optimizing what works today.
Full Requirements Documentation
Business rules, data and technology dependencies, potential role implications, and governance.
Prioritized V1 Capabilities
Sequenced backlog with defined V1 scope per initiative: what to build first, what to phase.
Week 1
Reimagine
Activities
  • Pain point validation with C&C, Paid Media, Brand, Legal, Localization, Agency
  • Identify reimagination vs optimization opportunities for agentic creative derivative production at scale
  • Set strategic framing and guardrails (scope, role coverage, AI ethics)
Deliverables
  • Data source map (AEM DAM, Brand Repo, Legal Rule Set, Translation Memory, Channel Specs)
  • Pain points tracked against current state
  • Current state mapped with future state solution
Week 2
Data and Tech Feasibility
Activities
  • Architecture feasibility solidified
  • Data sources clarified and tech / tools identified for future state
  • Finalize data, platform, and access needs with CIO
Deliverables
  • Draft reimagined initiative designs per initiative
  • Initial architecture requirements
  • Requirements documentation (business rules, data and tech dependencies, governance)
Week 3
Prioritization and Sequencing
Activities
  • Validate designs with functional leaders and process owners (C&C, Paid Media, Brand, Legal, Localization, Agency)
  • Prioritization stakeholder sign-off on initiatives and capability sequencing
  • Requirements for V1 scope finalized
Deliverables
  • Initial role impact assessment by domain
  • Sequenced capabilities to reach final state
Week 4
Finalize
Activities
  • Sequence V1 functionality / capabilities per initiative
  • Align on build-first vs phase vs defer
  • Final architecture sign-off with CIO
Deliverables
  • V1 scope definition with acceptance criteria
  • Architecture blueprint for build phase
  • Sequenced V1 backlog per initiative

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Activation dependent on readiness of Adobe control plane with agency activation systems and enabling Adobe-native plumbing.
  • Ad format prioritization needs definition (media spend + risk assessment).
  • Agency workflow integration: how do derivatives feed into activation?
  • Open: Ensure alignment with Paid Media team's use case.

KPIs

  • Campaign cycle time
  • Derivative creation time
  • CTR (near-term)
  • ROAS (longer-term)
  • Brand and legal exception rate
  • Efficiency gains across creative production
