Gap: Content Distribution & Channel Delivery

Content Distribution & Channel Delivery

Deliver

Multi-channel distribution workflow: after legal/compliance approval, assets are routed by type to Web (AEM/ibm.com), Email (Marketo), Social Media, Paid Media (AML), Events, and Seismic. Each channel has distinct format requirements, scheduling, and publishing tools. No initiative directly addresses the orchestration of content delivery across channels or centralized distribution dashboards. DAM does not notify stakeholders when assets are published (noted as open question in the process).

Note: This gap could be addressed by extending CC-CC-21 (Content Creation) to include the final delivery stage of the content lifecycle, or by creating a dedicated Content Distribution Orchestration initiative covering multi-channel scheduling, format adaptation, and publish notification.

Recommendations

Domain already exceeds 70% target
Target exceeded: Content & Creative achieves 95% coverage (18 of 19 processes mapped) — tied with Select Marketing for the highest coverage of any domain. CC-CC-21 (Content Creation) alone covers 12 processes spanning the entire production lifecycle.
  1. Close the final gap: Extend CC-CC-21 to include distribution orchestration
    Content Distribution is the natural next step after content creation. Expanding CC-CC-21's scope to include multi-channel delivery orchestration (AEM, Marketo, AMAL, Media Center, Seismic routing) would achieve 100% coverage.
    +1 process covered → 100%
  2. Prioritize review cycle automation within CC-CC-21
    The largest pain point across all content production processes is multi-round review cycles. Video Internal alone has 6 review gates for editing. Campaign Assets have 5+ sequential review stages. AI-assisted review consolidation, automated brand compliance checking, and smart routing could dramatically reduce the avg 2-month production cycle.
    Highest ROI AI intervention across 8 production processes
  3. Leverage CC-CC-22 to solve the 40+ asset type routing problem
    The Creative Brief Creation process routes to 40+ asset types through manual prioritization meetings. CC-CC-22's automated skill-matching against Workfront capacity data could replace the manual "What type of Asset is approved?" decision tree with intelligent workload allocation.
    Strategic: automates the critical routing bottleneck at the start of every production workflow
