    +19 processes → 58% coverage

    +14 processes → 72% coverage — this combination reaches 70% target
  4. New Initiative: CSR Compliance & Security Automation (+11 processes → 82%)

    +11 processes → 82% coverage
  5. New Initiative: Employee Impact & Volunteering Agent (+9 processes → 91%)
    Automate volunteer event sourcing, scheduling, communication, Goodera integration, campaign reporting, and gala/sponsorship management. Separate from grant lifecycle but significant operational overhead.
    +9 processes → 91% coverage
