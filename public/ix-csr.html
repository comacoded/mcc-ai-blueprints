Why v3 trim

105 → 15
  • Removes noise. The 105-process iX list bundled together core program workflows (grants, partner lifecycle, learning experiences) with low-leverage admin (vulnerability scans, charge-code creation, COM115 reviews, SCORM settings). The v3 trim keeps only the workflows that actually move the agentification needle.
  • Aligned to colleague's 8-bucket normalization. v3 preserves 7 of 8 buckets: IBM SkillsBuild (11), Geos & Markets, Giving & Volunteerism, Grants Execution, Responsible Business. Intentionally drops the Marketing (logo request) and Platforms & Technology (site maintenance) buckets.
  • Exposes the real gap. Coverage drops from an inflated 60% (where the denominator was padded by low-value admin) to an honest 33%. The remaining 10 gaps now point clearly at the initiatives CSR is missing: LX orchestration, content lifecycle, reporting/insights, volunteerism, and impact disclosure.
