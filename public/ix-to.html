Blueprint Initiatives

6 T&O initiatives from Master Excel

Spend and Performance Data Foundation

TO-TO-40
Automates PO data entry by ingesting SOW, quote, or contract data. Provides intelligent allocation guidance across tactics and quarters. Ingests downstream performance data to correlate spend with outcomes and produce ROI investment insights.
3 processes mapped

Budgeting and Planning

TO-TO-41
Develops optimal budgets and financial plans leveraging historical spend and performance data. Produces recommended budgets and plans with supporting insights to facilitate discussion and refinement with domain leaders.
5 processes mapped

Scenario Planning

TO-TO-42
Models financial and operational scenarios to deliver real-time insights and eliminate manual spreadsheet modeling. Supports faster investment planning and risk mitigation decisions with interactive "what-if" exploration.
3 processes mapped (shared with TO-41)

HC Optimization & Workforce Planning Copilot

TO-TO-43
Uses AI-assisted insights to optimize headcount planning and resource allocation by integrating hiring, attrition, and budget data into a single view. Guides optimal workforce mix based on outcomes and return on resource investment.
5 processes mapped

Tech Capability Intake

TO-TO-44
Centralizes technology capability request intake with guided discovery, governance review, and downstream workflow management. Reduces redundant procurement and increases visibility into existing tech capabilities across MCC.
1 process partially mapped

Agency Briefing Agent

TO-TO-45
Extends Event Briefer capabilities to prepare agency briefings from planning inputs in IPA. Agent auto-drafts briefings to request quotes from agencies and suppliers, populating IPA fields automatically.
1 process partially mapped

Recommendations to Reach 70%

3 options to close the 14-point gap
  1. New Initiative: Travel & Approval Automation
    Consolidate Operations and Investment travel processes into a single AI-driven approval workflow. Rule-based screening (staff:client ratio, overseas flagging, YoY attendance), AI-recommended attendees, automated HTRT-to-Concur flow. Builds on Amanda Reidy's existing AI approval work with Lucy. Covers 2 gap processes.
    +2 processes covered → 63% coverage
  2. New Initiative: People Ops Lifecycle Agent
    Covers onboarding automation (auto-learning assignment, channel provisioning), recognition workflow (nomination dashboard, budget reconciliation), and intern program coordination. Addresses 5 gap processes — the largest uncovered cluster. Low technical complexity (mostly rule-based triggers and workflow orchestration).
    +5 processes covered → 81% coverage (exceeds 70% target)
  3. Alternative: Expand Existing Initiatives
    Instead of new initiatives, broaden scope of TO-43 (HC Optimization) to include recruiting execution and onboarding, and expand TO-40 (Spend Foundation) to cover Wimbledon/Canada/Ambassador spend tracking. This avoids adding new initiative IDs but requires updating the Blueprint definitions. Could partially close the gap (+3-4 processes).
    +3-4 processes covered → 67-70% coverage (borderline)
Fastest path to 70%: Options 1 + 2 together cover +7 gap processes, bringing T&O from 56% to 81% — well above target. Even Option 2 alone (+5) reaches 74%.
