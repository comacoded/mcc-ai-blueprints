1 Process Overview

A dynamic, living account intelligence layer that aggregates internal and external signals (intent, firmographics, technology footprint, engagement history, pipeline status, buying group composition) and generates role-specific engagement guidance for any stakeholder at any moment. Replaces static, manually built playbooks with AI-generated guidance that auto-refreshes as signals change. Targets ABM at scale across 2,200+ named accounts.

3 End to End Process

Five stage closed loop. Signals flow in from source systems, are resolved into a unified account intelligence graph, transformed into role-specific playbooks by the AI authoring engine, delivered through stakeholder channels, and continuously refined by adoption and outcome data.

01

INGEST

Continuously aggregates intent signals, ISC pipeline / win-loss / partner data, Gong and SalesLoft call intelligence, Marketo engagement, and firmographic / technographic feeds into a unified account intelligence graph.

02

PROFILE

Builds and maintains living account profiles. Scores buying group maturity, maps stakeholder roles, identifies whitespace, tracks engagement velocity across the buying committee.

03

GENERATE

Produces role-specific playbooks per account or cohort: recommended content sequences, event invitations, talk tracks, competitive positioning, next-best-action guidance tailored to each stakeholder buying stage.

04

DISTRIBUTE

Delivers playbooks to sales, field marketing, and ABM teams through ISC dashboards, SalesLoft cadences, and Slack alerts. Auto-refreshes as underlying signals change.

05

LEARN

Tracks playbook adoption, downstream conversion, and deal outcomes. Feeds results back into the intelligence model to continuously refine recommendations and surface what actually moves deals.

6 Risks, Dependencies, and KPIs

Open Risks & Dependencies

  • Buying group composition incomplete in ISC for many accounts. Requires enrichment and validation before role-specific guidance is trustworthy.
  • People.ai integration must be treated as Horizon 2 input, not a V1 dependency, to avoid blocking pilot.
  • Account intelligence graph requires entity resolution across all sources; not yet operating at scale.
  • Vendor selection between 6Sense and Demandbase is open.
  • AI ethics, privacy, and brand voice review on generated content.

KPIs

  • Playbooks generated per quarter
  • Playbook assembly time (target: 50 to 70% reduction vs baseline)
  • Adoption rate among target FMs and DSs
  • Next-best-action acceptance rate
  • Deal lifecycle velocity (lead-to-opportunity, opportunity-to-close)
  • Signal freshness (time from source change to playbook refresh)
  • Quality / factuality score on generated content
